Hemi is my dog. He does a lot of things, some good some bad. Let's label his habits. Here are the controls:

  • You are free to drag and drop items with a mouse
  • Tab to progress through the Items
  • Shift + Tab to move backwards through the Items
  • Space to select an Item
  • Escape to de-select an Item
  • Control + M to change the focus outside of a listbox
  • Enter to drop an Item in desired listbox
  • Shift + Space to select multiple Items at once
